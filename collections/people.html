People¶

This collection describes the members of the research group. This is normally public data.

Schema¶

The following lists key names mapped to its type and meaning for each entry.

_id

string, unique identifier for the group member, required

active

boolean, If the person is an active member, default True., optional

aka

[‘string’, ‘list’], list of aliases (also-known-as), useful for identifying the group member in citations or elsewhere., required

avatar

string, URL to avatar, required

string, short biographical text, required
